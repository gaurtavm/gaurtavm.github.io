Bio

Gaurav Gautam is currently a PhD student at the University of Minnesota . He completed his M.Tech degree in Communication and Networks with the guidance of Dr. Parimal Parag from the Indian Institute of Science , Bangalore in 2019. Gaurav Gautam holds a B.Tech degree in Electronics and Communication engineering from the Dronacharya College of Engineering Gurugram completed in 2015.

Research Interests

Computer networks, Data center networking, Software defined networks, Stochastic processes, Blockchains, Machine learning, etc.
